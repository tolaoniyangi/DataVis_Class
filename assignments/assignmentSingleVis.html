REGISTERED INDIGENOUS VICTIMS OF THE COLOMBIA CONFLICT (1984-2015): 191 indigenous people registered as victims of the conflict for 1985, and 17,776 indigenous people registered for 2005. These years have the highest and lowest number of reported victims. The majority of victims reported displacement as the type of crime they suffered (69% of people for 1985, and 96% for 2005)
